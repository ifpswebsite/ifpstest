About us

IFPS is a locally owned fire protection company in Nogales, Arizona, with over 25 years of experience. Founded by former firefighters, we provide reliable and affordable fire protection for homes and businesses. We specialize in the installation, maintenance, and inspection of fire extinguishers, sprinkler systems, and commercial kitchen fire suppression systems. Our professional, knowledgeable team ensures your property is protected to the highest standards, giving you peace of mind.

Fire Extinguishers

We install and maintain a comprehensive range of fire extinguishers suited to your business needs, ensuring compliance with industry standards and delivering reliable, high-quality protection.

Fire Supression Systems

We provide installation and maintenance of fire suppression systems, including sprinklers, backflow systems, and kitchen fire suppression systems, ensuring reliable protection for your business

Service & Maintenance

We provide inspections, maintenance and support to keep all systems fully operational and compliant with fire protection standards, with 24/7 emergency service when you need it most.
