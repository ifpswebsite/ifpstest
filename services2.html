Fire Extinguishers

We supply, install, and maintain a full range of fire extinguishers suitable for every type of fire risk. This includes ABC multipurpose extinguishers for general use, BC extinguishers for flammable liquids and electrical fires, Class K extinguishers for commercial kitchens, and other specialized units for industrial or high-risk applications. All our products meet safety standards and are backed by professional servicing to ensure long-term reliability and compliance.

Fire Supression Systems

We provide professional installation, maintenance, and inspection of comprehensive fire suppression systems to protect your property and ensure compliance with industry standards. Our services include sprinkler systems and commercial kitchen suppression systems designed for both safety and efficiency. All of our systems are installed to meet Specialty Dual Fire Protection Systems standards and UL 300 requirements, ensuring the highest level of fire safety and reliability. Whether for homes, offices, or commercial kitchens, our team delivers solutions that are fully compliant with NFPA and Arizona fire codes, giving you peace of mind that your property is protected around the clock.

Fire Alarms & Emergency Lighting

We provide professional installation, inspection, and maintenance of fire alarm systems and emergency lighting for both homes and businesses. These systems are essential for early detection, safe evacuation, and fast response, helping to protect people and property. Our team ensures every installation meets code requirements and is tailored to your needs, giving you complete peace of mind.

Backflow Systems

Backflow prevention is a critical part of fire protection systems. These devices stop stagnant sprinkler water from flowing back into the main water supply, helping maintain safety and compliance. We provide professional installation, testing, and maintenance of backflow systems to ensure your fire sprinklers function properly and meet all code requirements.
